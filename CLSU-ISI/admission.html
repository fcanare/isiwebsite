Admission Requirements

  1. With passing grades in MATH 100, COMSCI 100, and MATH 105
  2. GPA of 2.25 or better
  3. Must pass the qualifying interview in the department.
  4. Applicant who fails to satisfy the GPA requirement but whose records, in the opinion of the screening committee, shows some promise will be given probationary status.
  5. Duly accomplished BSIT-application form.

Transferees and shifters seeking admission into the program must comply with the existing University policies regarding transfer or shifting

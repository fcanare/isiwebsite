About Us

BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Objectives of the program

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology(BSIT) is a 4-year-program offered by the Department of Information Technology, College of Engineering in partnership with the Information Systems Institute at the Central Luzon State University. It is designed to produce Information Technology professionals who are ready to pursue careers in the various fields of computing and Information Technology. Students are trained to plan, design, develop, implement, and maintain   Information Technology systems. Thus, graduates are equipped with necessary skills and are prepared to be part of the industry or become competent members of the ICT/MIS department of corporations.

The BSIT program is envisioned to specifically produce graduates who are:

  • Trained extensively in abstract and analytical processes;
  • Equipped with technical skills affording them maximum flexibility;
  • Grounded in appropriate concepts and principles;
  • Adaptive to changing work environment to function effectively in multi-disciplinary and diverse team;
  • Capable to understand all aspects of design process, taking into account functional and human considerations; including environmental, legal, ethical, and societal implications of Information Technology projects.
  • Adept with the sense of professionalism and the personal discipline to succeed in their chosen career guided by social and moral values.

The program presently offers two specializations from which students can choose to take on their senior year;

  1. BSIT-Systems Development(BSIT-SysDev) Majors can fulfill careers as:
  • Web Administrator
  • Web Master
  • Web Developer
  • Web Applications Developer
  • Software Applications Developer
  • Systems Programmer
  • Database Administrator
  • Database Analyst
  • Systems Analyst
  • Technical Support Specialist
  • Systems Support Specialist
  • Information Systems Especialist
  • Systems Administrators
  1. BSIT-Networking (BSIT-Networking) Majors can pursue careers as:
  • Network Administrator
  • Network Specialist
  • Software Applications Developer
  • Systems Programmer
  • Database Administrator
  • Database Analyst
  • Systems Analyst
  • Technical Support Specialist
  • Systems Support Specialist
  • Information Systems Specialist
  • Systems Administrator
